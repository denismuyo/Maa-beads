The Story Behind Our Beads

Each Maasai beadwork piece carries deep cultural significance. The colors and patterns tell stories of heritage, status, and life transitions.

Red

Symbolizes bravery, strength, and unity. Worn by warriors and leaders.

Blue

Represents the sky and energy. Signifies sustenance and the divine.

Green

Symbolizes health, land, and prosperity. Worn during harvest celebrations.
